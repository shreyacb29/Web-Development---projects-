Cinnamon Roll Recipe


Ingredients:


  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:


  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter


Instructions

  1. Mix the milk yeast sugar eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
